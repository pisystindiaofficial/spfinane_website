Privacy Policy

At SP Enterprises (“we”, “our”, “us”), your privacy and data protection are of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website, mobile application, or any other service offered by us (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Purpose of This Policy

This policy aims to ensure transparency and accountability regarding how we handle your personal information. By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please discontinue using our Services immediately.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect both personal and non-personal information depending on how you interact with us. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Personal details such as name, date of birth, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Identity verification details such as PAN, Aadhaar number, Voter ID, or Driving License for compliance purposes.
  • Financial details such as income proof, bank account numbers, salary slips, or credit history for loan processing.
  • Device information, IP address, browser details, and usage analytics to improve service performance.
  • Communication records, including emails, messages, or voice interactions with our representatives.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • To verify your identity and eligibility for financial products.
  • To process loan applications, disbursements, and repayments.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements such as KYC and AML regulations.
  • To communicate updates, service notifications, and account-related alerts.
  • To improve the performance and usability of our website and mobile app.
  • To prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or misuse of our systems.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information under one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • To perform our contractual obligations related to financial products or services.
  • To comply with statutory and regulatory obligations imposed by Indian law.
  • To pursue legitimate business interests while protecting user rights and freedoms.
  • When we have obtained your explicit consent for specific processing activities.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, to comply with legal and regulatory obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements. Data may be archived or stored securely even after account closure for audit or compliance purposes as required by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) or other authorities.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal information. However, we may share data in the following cases:

  • With our trusted business partners, NBFCs, or banking institutions involved in providing financial products.
  • With third-party service providers (like KYC verification agencies, credit bureaus, or payment gateways) who assist in delivering our Services.
  • With regulators, law enforcement agencies, or courts when required under applicable law.
  • During corporate restructuring, merger, or acquisition, where data may be transferred to the new entity under confidentiality obligations.

7. Data Security and Storage

We employ advanced technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission and storage.
  • Secure access controls and authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular system audits, security scans, and penetration testing.
  • Restricted employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

While we strive to maintain the highest standards of security, no online platform can guarantee absolute safety. Users are encouraged to maintain strong passwords and refrain from sharing confidential credentials with anyone.

8. Your Rights and Choices

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access and review your personal information stored with us.
  • The right to request corrections or updates to inaccurate data.
  • The right to withdraw consent, where processing is based on consent.
  • The right to request deletion of personal information, subject to legal limitations.

To exercise these rights, you can write to our support team at our official contact channel mentioned on the website.

9. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and app use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can choose to disable cookies through browser settings; however, doing so may limit certain functionalities.

10. Third-Party Services

Our Services may include integrations with third-party tools such as payment gateways, analytics platforms, and KYC partners. These third-party entities operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their terms before engaging with their services through our platform.

11. Protection of Minors

SP Enterprises does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18 years of age. If it comes to our attention that we have inadvertently received such data, we will promptly delete it from our systems.

We may periodically revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our operations, technologies, or legal obligations. The updated version will be posted on our website with a “Last Updated” date. You are encouraged to review this page periodically to stay informed about our data practices.
