Once a payment is successfully made through our website, mobile application, or payment gateway, it is deemed final. Cancellations of confirmed transactions are not permitted unless explicitly specified under exceptional conditions, such as:

Refunds, if applicable, will be processed only under genuine and verified cases as per our internal verification process. Once approved, the refund will be credited back to the original mode of payment within 7 to 10 working days.
